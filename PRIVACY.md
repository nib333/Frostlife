# Privacy Policy — Frostlife

Frostlife does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

- **No network access.** The app has no internet permission and makes no
  network requests of any kind — not for analytics, not for updates, not
  for anything.
- **No accounts.** There is no sign-in, no user identifier, and nothing
  tied to you personally.
- **All data stays on your device.** Game state, settings, and stats
  (player names and win/loss records you choose to record) are stored
  locally on your phone and never leave it.
- **No third parties.** Nothing is shared with Jolla, the developer, or
  anyone else. There is no telemetry or crash reporting.

You can delete recorded stats at any time with the in-app "Clear stats"
option on the Stats page. Saved game state and settings live in your
user configuration database on the device; they never leave it, and
starting a new game or reinstalling the app overwrites them.
